Instructions on deleting your account: 

Any user can delete their account by navigating to the settings option in the side navigation bar. 

Then, there will be a delete button which upon pressing deletes the users account and credentials that are stored with the app. 

For the users who signed in with guest account need not delete their account as no information is stored within the app.
